Questions

Fair questions, straight answers

Is DietAisle free?

Yes. Meal planning, price comparison, receipt and barcode scanning are all free, with no ads. You just need a free account so your data can follow you across devices.

Where does my data live?

In your account. Your plans, product database and prices live in your own row in the database and sync to every device you sign in on. Your device keeps a copy so the app still works offline.

Which supermarkets does it cover?

Price comparison covers the big UK names — Lidl, Aldi, Tesco, ASDA, Sainsbury's, Morrisons and more — and learns your local prices from the receipts you scan.

Does it track my location?

Only when you tap "find stores near me", and only for that one lookup. There's no background tracking — the app can't do it even if it wanted to, and it doesn't want to.

How do I install it on my phone?

Open the app in your browser, then: on iPhone, tap Share → "Add to Home Screen". On Android, tap the install prompt or menu → "Install app". It then opens full-screen like any other app and works offline.
