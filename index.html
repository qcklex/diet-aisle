How it works

Three steps, once a week

1 · Plan

Generate the week

Set your macros or pick a preset. The AI builds seven days of meals that hit your numbers.

2 · Shop

Follow the verdict

One list, priced per store. DietAisle tells you the cheapest shop near you and what you save.

3 · Scan

Close the loop

Scan the receipt on the way out. Real prices and nutrition flow back into next week's plan.

Eat to your goals. Keep the difference.
